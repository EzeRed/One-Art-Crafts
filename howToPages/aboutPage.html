About me

Hi, my name is Ezequiel. I am from Durham, Nc and I am the founder of One Art Crafts. When I was in elementry, I was a kid who liked to make thing with paper. The school days were boring and repitive. Doing the same every day. Math, Science, Language Arts, Social Studies. I wanted to do something else. With all this notebook paper, Let's make crafts. Years later I decided to make a website about Origami Crafts and how to make them. I started with simple crafts like paper planes and boats, but soon I moved on to more complex designs like origami animals and flowers. As I continued to create and experiment with different designs, I realized that origami was not just a hobby, but a way to express myself and bring joy to others. That's when I decided to share my passion with the world and create One Art Crafts.
