Origami Boat

Intro to Origami Boat

October 21, 2024

Welcome to the ultimate guide on crafting the classic paper boat! Whether you're a novice looking to dip your toes into the world of origami or an expert seeking a bit of nostalgic fun, you're in the right place. Here, you'll find easy-to-follow steps, tips, and a touch of creativity to make your paper boat building journey smooth and enjoyable. So grab some paper, unleash your inner artist, and let's set sail on this whimsical adventure together!

Materials required for Origami Boat

  • 1 rectangle piece of paper (preferably origami paper)
  • Scissors (optional)

Instructions


Step 1: Get what you need:

  • You only need one piece of origami paper to make a Origami Boat

Step 2: Make a hat shaped paper

  • Start with a rectangular piece of paper. Fold it in half lengthwise, then unfold to create a crease.
  • Fold it in half widthwise, with the creased edge at the top.
  • The paper should have a cross crease mark on the paper
  • Fold the top corners towards the center to make two diagonal creases that meet at the center.
  • Fold the bottom edge of the front flap up, aligning with the base of the triangles.
  • Flip it over.
  • You will see two corners popping out from the previous fold.
  • Fold the little corners inwards to make the little corners as shown in the picture below.
  • Fold the bottom edge of the front flap up just like the front flap.
  • Now, you have a hat shaped paper

Step 3: Diamond shape

  • Pull the two outer flaps apart gently, it should be a diamond shape
  • When you finish pulling, the paper should be flat, Make sure it completely flat. Press around the whole edge of paper so ti stays flat. completely flat and all the edges are folded.
  • Take the botton point flap To the top corners of the diamond shape.
  • Fold the layer to the top of the edge.
  • Flip the paper over. Take the bottom point to the top of the edge of the paper.
  • It is now a triangle shape.

Step 4: Finishing Folds

  • Pull the two outer flaps apart gently, it should be a diamond shape.
  • When you finish pulling, the paper should be flat again. Press on it the paper if necessary
  • Now at the top opening of the paper, Pull the two corners outwards. It slowing start making the boat shape
  • If it doesn't correctly, make adjustments
  • Look at botton of the boat and pinched edges to get the boat to stand

Congratulations!

  • You made a paper boat!
