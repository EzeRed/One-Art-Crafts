Origami Ninja Star

Intro to Origami Ninja Star:

October 21, 2024

Hi there, did you come here to learn how to make a origami ninja star? A origami ninja star is one of the most made origami designs. During my elementry and middle school days kids were always messing with papers, making paper air planes, paper hat, and many others. The ninja star were too good to make, that why it was so popular. Maybe you seen your friends with a paper ninja star and caught your interest in making one.

They are easy to make with our step by step instructions below. It is so easy to make that any kid or adult make one. Now. let get started!


Materials required for Origami Ninja Star:

  • 2 square pieces of oriami paper (preferably origami paper)
  • Scissors

Instructions


Step 1: Get what you need:

  • You only need two pieces of origami paper to make a ninja star

Step 2: Fold each paper into a triangle:

  • Folld each paper and fold it from top to bottom.
  • You should have a half folded paper
  • Next, open the fold paper back up and fold the top corner and bottom corners down to the middle crease.
  • Fold the paper in half right again on the first center fold
  • This is the same as thefirst step, take the bottom edge to the top.
  • Noew, you have a long triangle shaped paper. Fold the paper in half for left to right. (Essentially, you are folding the paper in half again)
  • Now have a small triangle

Step 3: Fold the ends in opposite directions:

  • Open the paper back up and fold the botton right edge down to the middle crease.
  • Next, get the other left edge and fold it up to the middle crease. Both ennds should be looking at the opposite direction

Step 4:Fold the both corners into triangles

  • Bare with me, this is the tricky part. Flip the paper where the middle part is showing and Fold the bottom outer edge end of the paper to the middle edge lined. It should look like a triangle.
  • Do the same for the other side
  • Now with both end looking like triangles, fold the bottom and up triangle ends up to the middle
  • But leave them there, this is only to crease the fold.
  • Now, do all the same steps for the second paper.
  • Now both papers like a Z shapes. Now we combine them!

Step 5: Combine the two papers:

  • Take the blue one and have on it top of red the where the blue one having the middle part showing
  • Now, get the top endtriangle of the red paper and fold it and slip it inside the middle line of the blue paper.
  • Do the same for the bottom end triangle
  • Now, flip the nealy fininshed paper ninja star over.
  • You are preety much doing the same thing as before. Sliping the blue paper into the middle line of red paper
  • This time it may harder slip it through so really push the end in there. You may have to lossen the paper so it can go in

Congratulations!

  • You made a paper ninja star!
